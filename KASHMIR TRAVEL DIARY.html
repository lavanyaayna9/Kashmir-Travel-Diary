TRAVEL EXPERIENCES

Our journey to Kashmir began with a warm welcome aboard a beautifully adorned houseboat on Dal Lake in Srinagar. The serene waters, framed by the majestic Himalayas, provided a tranquil and picturesque setting. As evening fell, we enjoyed a peaceful Shikara ride, gliding past floating gardens and vibrant markets, all while witnessing a breathtaking sunset that painted the sky in warm hues.

The next day, we ventured to Sonmarg, the "Meadow of Gold," known for its stunning landscapes. The drive itself was an adventure, offering views of lush valleys and snow-capped peaks. In Sonmarg, we took a thrilling gondola ride to higher altitudes, where the sight of pristine glaciers and the cool mountain breeze left us in awe.

Gulmarg, our next destination, is a haven for adventure enthusiasts. The gondola ride to the Apharwat peak was an unforgettable experience, offering panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. We spent the day exploring the snow-covered landscapes, making snowmen, and engaging in friendly snowball fights.

Our final destination was Pahalgam, where we immersed ourselves in the tranquility of Aru and Betaab valleys. These picturesque valleys, with their lush green meadows and crystal-clear streams, provided the perfect setting for leisurely strolls and picnics. The local markets in Pahalgam were a delight to explore, offering unique handicrafts and traditional Kashmiri attire.
